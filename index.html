Please allow browser to see your geolocation

iOS:
1．Open iOS Settings
2．Choose Privacy > Location Services. If the Location Services is disabled, turn it on.
3．Choose Privacy > Location Services > Safari Websites
4．Choose While Using the App under Allow Location Access

Chrome on Desktop:
1．Click the three dots on the upper right corner of the Chrome browser.
2．Click Settings, then Security and Privacy.
3．Click Site settings.
4．Click Location and check “Sites can ask for your location” is on.
5．If checkyouruv.vercel.com is listed under “Not allowed to see your location”, click Permission > Location > Ask or Allow.

Safari on Desktop:
1．Open Safari > Preferences > Websites > Location
2．If checkyouruv.vercel.com is denied to access your location, click Ask or Allow.
